# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 9, 2026  
**Service:** phishXzero

This Privacy Policy explains how phishXzero collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

- Account data: username, email, authentication provider.
- Security analysis data: submitted email content, URL inputs, file scan metadata/results.
- Application logs: scan timestamps, verdicts, risk scores, technical diagnostics.
- Google account data (optional): profile details and Gmail metadata/content only when you connect Google and grant permission.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Authenticate users and manage accounts.
- Provide phishing, URL, and malware detection services.
- Operate Gmail Shield workflows (inbox scan, label/quarantine actions) when enabled by user.
- Maintain audit logs, troubleshoot issues, and improve service reliability.

## 3. Google API Services and Limited Use

If you authorize Google access:

- phishXzero accesses Google data only for user-requested features.
- Google user data is not sold.
- Google user data is not used for advertising.
- Google user data is not shared except as required to provide the feature, by law, or with user consent.

phishXzero’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the **Google API Services User Data Policy**, including Limited Use requirements.

## 4. Data Retention

- Account and scan data are retained while your account remains active, unless deletion is requested.
- Logs and analysis records may be retained for security, audit, and operational purposes.
- Google OAuth tokens are stored to maintain authorized connectivity and can be revoked by user.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.  
Data may be shared only in these cases:

- Service providers or infrastructure required to operate the Service.
- Legal compliance, court order, or law enforcement request.
- Protection of rights, security, and abuse prevention.

## 6. Security Measures

We apply reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect stored data. No system is fully immune to risk, and users should follow standard account-security practices.

## 7. User Controls and Rights

You may:

- Access and update account information.
- Disconnect Google account access from your Google Account settings.
- Request deletion of your account and associated data (subject to lawful retention obligations).

## 8. Children’s Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13 (or equivalent local age threshold), and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. International Data Handling

If used across regions, data may be processed/stored in jurisdictions with different data protection laws.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
